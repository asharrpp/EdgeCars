Services Overview

Booking

This is our basic and most important feature. Customers will be able to book cabs anytime and from anywhere using our website or toll-free number. They will have to specify the pickup and drop location while booking.

Sharing

This will allow customers to share their wides with others who have the same destination/pick up location. Customers opting for this will have to pay a lower price as compared to booking the whole cab.

Renting

Here the customers can rent the car for 6 hours (or more by allowing extensions). Before renting the customerâ€™s address and mobile number will be verified. In case of any damages to the vehicle, appropriate charges will be levied from him/her.

Drive with us

Here people can opt to be employed by us as drivers. Driver salary will be based on the number of successful trips and the average monthly rating given to him/her by the customers.

Membership plan

By giving a monthly fare, customers will be able to afford more expensive rides at a discounted price throughout the month. This would be cancellable at any time but not refundable.

Freight

Freight services will allow large-scale companies to transport goods in bulk by trucks. To apply for this companies will have to reach out to us using the details given in the contact section. Only authorized carriers will be allowed to act as drivers in this service.
